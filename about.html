Our Story

A Journey Built on
Craftsmanship, Trust & Growth

RKN Associates began its journey in 2005 with a simple vision — to deliver strong, reliable, and high-quality work that people can trust. What started as a focused stainless steel fabrication concern has grown into a recognised name across Tamil Nadu.

Our first project was at New Bharathi Raja Hospital, T.Nagar — a milestone that set the standard for everything that followed. From that first installation, we built relationships with leading architects, engineers, and builders who trusted us to bring their visions to life.

Over the years, we have worked on hospitals, star hotels, celebrity homes, government institutions, showrooms, and landmark commercial spaces. We introduced the Color Diamond Model in Tamil Nadu — a design innovation that the industry has yet to replicate.

"From humble beginnings to a trusted name — our journey is built on quality and consistency."

Today, RKN Associates stands as a symbol of trust, combining experience with innovation to deliver work that stands — literally and figuratively — for decades.

Milestones

Two Decades, at a Glance

2005
Founded in Triplicane

S.S.M. Hussain Sahib establishes RKN Associates as a stainless steel fabrication firm in Triplicane, Chennai, with a focus on quality materials and craftsmanship.

2006
First Landmark Project

Delivered the stainless steel handrail installation at New Bharathi Raja Hospital, T.Nagar, in February 2006 — the project that set the standard for everything that followed.

2007
Actor Vishal's House, Anna Nagar

Completed the stainless steel work at Tamil film star Vishal's residence — RKN's first celebrity installation and a showcase of the Wood-Steel Western signature model.

2009
Star Achiever Award

First state-level recognition. Awarded at German Hall, T.Nagar, with Gold Medal for consistent quality and service by the State Human Rights Vigilance Organisation.

2010
Color Diamond Model Debut

Introduced the Color Diamond Model at La Chocolate Patisserie, Egmore — the first installation of this signature design in Tamil Nadu. The same year, RKN received the Bharat Ratna Dr. MGR Award.

2012
Viswa Jothi Award — Third Consecutive Recognition

Third state-level award in four years, reaffirming RKN's standing across the industry for material integrity and dependable execution.

2013
Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Family Residence, Pamban

Selected to deliver stainless steel work at the family residence of the Missile Man of India — the project RKN holds closest to its heart.

2026
Digital Relaunch & Next Chapter

After two decades of quiet, word-of-mouth growth, RKN Associates launches a modern digital presence — bringing the same standard to a new generation of architects, builders, and homeowners.
