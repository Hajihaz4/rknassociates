Quick Answers

Questions Before You Call?

Do you handle projects outside Chennai?
Yes — we've completed projects across Tamil Nadu including Rameswaram, Nagore, Kilakarai, Pondicherry, Coimbatore and more. We travel for the work.
What is the typical project timeline?
Timeline depends on scope. A standard residential handrail takes 1–2 weeks from measurement to installation. Larger commercial projects vary. We give a clear timeline after the site visit.
Do you offer site visits?
Yes — site visits within Chennai are free. For projects outside the city, travel costs are discussed upfront. Either way — we see the site before we quote.
What materials do you work with?
We work with 304, 310, 316, 321, 410, and 430 grade stainless steel — selected based on the application. For exterior and marine environments we recommend 316; for interiors 304 is standard.
Is there a minimum project size?
No strict minimum — but we focus on quality over volume. Whether it's a short staircase handrail or a full-building cladding project, we give each the same attention to detail.
