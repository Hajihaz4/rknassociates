Who We Are

21 Years. Hundreds of Projects.
One Standard — Excellence.

RKN Associates was founded in 2005 with a single purpose — to bring international quality stainless steel craftsmanship to Tamil Nadu. From our first project at New Bharathi Raja Hospital, T.Nagar, we have never compromised on material quality, design precision, or delivery commitment.

We were the first to introduce the Color Diamond Model in Tamil Nadu — a design innovation that redefined what stainless steel could look like. Trusted by leading architects, recognised by the Government of Tamil Nadu.
